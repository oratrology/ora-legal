## FREE TRIAL
Renews automatically unless cancelled 24h before end. One trial per Apple ID/Google account/email.

## AUTO-RENEWAL
Subscriptions renew automatically. Continues until cancelled.

## PRE-RENEWAL NOTIFICATIONS
- 7 days before: reminder with amount
- 24h before: final reminder (annual plans)
- 30 days: advance notice for price changes

## HOW TO CANCEL
iOS: Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → ORA → Cancel
Android: Play Store → Profile → Payments → Subscriptions → ORA → Cancel
Web: Account Settings → Subscription → Cancel
⚠️ DELETING THE APP DOES NOT CANCEL YOUR SUBSCRIPTION

## EFFECT OF CANCELLATION
Access continues to end of paid period. No further charges. Account reverts to ORA Free. Reading history preserved. Astral Rewards balance maintained.

## FAILED PAYMENTS
Day 1: First attempt | Day 3: Second | Day 7: Third | Day 8: Downgrade to ORA Free

## REFUNDS BY PLATFORM
Apple: reportaproblem.apple.com
Google: Within 48h via Play Store; after 48h → support@oratrology.com
Web (Stripe/PayPal): Within 14 days → support@oratrology.com
Gumroad: Subject to Gumroad policy + ORA policy

## STATUTORY RIGHTS
EU/Spain: 14-day withdrawal right (exception: service started with consent)

Brazil: 7-day CDC withdrawal right
UK: 14-day cooling-off period
EU ODR: ec.europa.eu/consumers/odr

## NON-REFUNDABLE
Completed readings, redeemed Astral Rewards, expired subscriptions, renewals after deadline, promotional credits.

## ASTRAL REWARDS
No monetary value. Non-transferable. Expire on account termination.

## PASSIVE CAMERA FEATURE NOTE
The passive camera / emotional analysis feature used during readings does not affect refund eligibility. Refunds processed normally per purchase platform regardless of camera permissions.
